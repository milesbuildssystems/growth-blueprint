Where every buyer sits, and what moves them up.

Every buyer who could hire ANW sits on one of five rungs. The mistake is talking to all of them the same way. Here is what each rung believes, and the one thing that moves them up to the next.

UnawareCold
What they believe right now

“What we have works, mostly.” They are not looking, because nothing has blown up recently.

What moves them up

A reminder of what a failure actually costs, a missed STAT run, a broken cold chain, a compliance flag, so “mostly” stops feeling safe.

Problem-awareCool
What they believe right now

“Our courier situation is a liability. I just have not found a better option I trust.”

What moves them up

Show them a courier built for exactly this, credentialed, compliant, reachable, so the safer option finally exists.

Solution-awareWarming
What they believe right now

“Plenty of couriers say they do medical. Which ones can actually prove it?”

What moves them up

Lead with proof they can verify, TWIC, TSA, DOT, HIPAA aware, insurance, references, so ANW is the one that clears the bar.

Product-awareWarm
What they believe right now

“I like what I have heard about ANW. I just need it to be easy to start.”

What moves them up

A clear next step, a quote form and a fast response, plus a small pilot lane to prove it with low risk.

Most-awareHot
What they believe right now

“Send me the quote and let us run a lane.”

What moves them up

One page, one form, a fast reply. This is the buyer your quote system should reach first.
