The awareness ladder · prepared for Maya Rivera

Where every buyer sits, and what moves them up.

Every person who lands on your content sits on one of five rungs. The mistake is talking to all of them the same way. Here is what each rung believes, and the one thing that moves them up to the next.

UnawareCold
What they believe right now

“I eat fine, I just get busy sometimes.” She is not looking for a solution because she has not named the problem yet.

What moves them up

A reel that names the pattern out loud, the strong start then the week-two fade, so she thinks “wait, that is me.”

Problem-awareCool
What they believe right now

“I always fall off. Maybe I just do not have the discipline.” She blames herself, not the missing system.

What moves them up

Show her it is the missing plan, not her willpower. That reframe is what makes her want what you have.

Solution-awareWarming
What they believe right now

“Plans work for other people. The ones I tried did not stick.” She is comparing approaches and a little skeptical.

What moves them up

Show why yours survives a real week (fast, high-protein, no three-hour Sunday) when the others did not.

Product-awareWarm
What they believe right now

“I like her stuff, I will get the program eventually.” Warm, but no reason to act today.

What moves them up

A clear, simple offer with a nudge to start now, plus proof from someone just like her.

Most-awareHot
What they believe right now

“Just tell me where to click.” She is sold on you, she needs the path.

What moves them up

One page, one button, no friction. This is the person your captured list should hit first.

Meet each person where they actually are, and more of them climb toward a yes.
