The awareness ladder · prepared for Brittney Batla

Where every buyer sits, and what moves them up.

Every person who lands on your Reels sits on one of five rungs. The mistake is talking to all of them the same way. Here is what each rung believes, and the one thing that moves them up to the next.

UnawareCold
What they believe right now

“My business is fine, I will get to Instagram when I have time.” She has not connected slow growth to the missing system, so there is nothing to solve yet.

What moves them up

A Reel that makes the real cost concrete (what inconsistent posting is quietly costing her in leads), so “later” starts to feel expensive.

Problem-awareCool
What they believe right now

“I am just bad at this content thing.” She blames herself, not the missing system.

What moves them up

Show her it is the missing system, not her discipline. That reframe is what makes her want your AI approach.

Solution-awareWarming
What they believe right now

“AI works for tech people. I am not that person.” Comparing, skeptical it will work for her.

What moves them up

Show why your AI Reel approach works for a busy non-technical owner, not a prompt engineer.

Product-awareWarm
What they believe right now

“I trust Brittney. I will grab the coaching call or Academy when I am ready.” Warm, but no reason to act today.

What moves them up

A clear, simple offer with a first step she can take today, plus proof from someone who started where she is.

Most-awareHot
What they believe right now

“Just tell me what to do first.” Sold on you, she needs the path.

What moves them up

One page, one button, no friction. The first thing your captured list should see.
