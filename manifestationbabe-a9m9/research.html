Who's really following you

Who is actually on the other side of that podcast.

This is who binges your podcast on their commute and screenshots the affirmations. Not a demographic guess, the person your content already speaks to.

The woman who knows she is meant for more
Your core buyer
  • Late 20s to 40s, ambitious, often juggling a job or business she has outgrown, quietly certain her current life is not the ceiling.
  • Follows you because you make abundance feel like a skill she can learn, not woo-woo luck reserved for other people.
  • Has already read a manifestation book, tried journaling, maybe done a challenge. Felt the shift, then daily life pulled her back. Quietly wonders why it stuck for you and not her.
  • Saves your episodes and reels constantly. Rarely takes the next step, because there is no next step waiting for her.
What they are really buying: not another course. They are buying “I can finally become the version of me I keep glimpsing.” The relief of a practice and a community that keeps her in it when motivation fades.

What your content is doing

Your engine, and the intent it is leaving on the table.

Your top format

Intimate, high-energy podcast episodes where you teach a mindset shift like a friend who has been there. That is your engine. It travels because it converts inside their headphones, one-on-one, at scale.

What gets saved and sent

Your breakthrough episodes and affirmation reels get saved and sent to a friend. A save is a promise to come back. A share is a referral. Both are buying intent.

Where that intent goes

Nowhere you own. The listener finishes the episode and moves on, the book buyer disappears into Amazon, and none of it lands anywhere you can follow up. That is the gap this blueprint closes.

The path a new fan takes right now

I traced it step by step. Here is where it breaks.

1

Finds an episode. Binges three more on the drive home. She is fully in now, this is the warmest she will ever be.

2

Looks you up, finds your Instagram, taps the link in your bio.

3

Lands on a product page and considers the Academy or a book.

4

There is no free first step and no email ask, just buy-now buttons on a warm-up-less page. She is obsessed but not ready to spend today, so she tells herself she will come back. She never does, and there is nothing to bring her back.

5

You never got her email. Next week you record another episode to go find her, and someone just like her, all over again.

The break

It is between the visit and the buy. Every warm listener who hits a buy-now page with no free first step and no email ask is gone forever. One page, one ask, one thing she wants, and that step stops leaking.
